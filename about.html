Overview

TaskEase is a simple task management platform designed to streamline your tasks. Whether you're a busy professional, a student, or a homemaker, TaskEase helps you organize your tasks efficiently.

Key Features

  • Intuitive task creation and management
  • Speech-to-text functionality for hands-free task entry
  • Integrated chat for seamless communication

Instructions to use TaskEase

  • To add a task through Speech
    • Click on 'Start Recording' and say your task.
    • Set date & time for Task Reminders. Also set a priority for the task.
    • Click on 'Save Task' to save the task.
  • To add a task through Chat
    • If you want to add a new task, type: "add task" followed by the task name, date, and time.
      For example, to add a task called 'Finish report' on June 8, 2024, at 2:00 PM, you would type: "add task Finish report on 8th June 2pm".
    • If you want to view all your tasks, simply type: "view". You can also Delete and Update your tasks.
  • To view your Saved Tasks
    • Navigate to 'View Tasks' section in the Toggle Menu to view all your tasks.
    • You can also Delete and Update your tasks.
    • In the 'Profile' section of the Toggle Menu, click on the highlighted dates in the calendar to see the overview of tasks.
